Who We Are

A Name You Can Trust
In Jefferson Parish

We're not a national franchise. Young's Roofing & Exteriors Co. is a local, owner-operated business built on the principle that your home deserves the same care and attention we'd give our own.

Our crew lives and works in Metairie, Kenner, and River Ridge. We understand Louisiana weather — the humidity, the heat, and the devastating storms — and we build roofs specifically designed to withstand it all.
